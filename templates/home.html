About Tutor++

Tutor++ is a web applicaton designed and deployed by Technopolis. It is a tutor web application that connects tutors and students online. It allows departments to post positions and students to apply for said positions. Tutor++ is a web-app that helps DUT Staff and students find each other in a designated environment for faster and more efficient communication. It is founded, developed and deployed by Technopolis.
